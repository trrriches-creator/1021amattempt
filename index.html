Top 5 Seasons of Survivor

  1. Survivor: David vs Goliath (Season 37)

    • Best fresh cast of all time
    • Perfectly fleshed-out theme
    • Engaging challenges
    • Interesting story lines for full cast
    • Fantastic strategic gameplay

  2. Survivor: Cagayan (Season 28)

    • Another dynamic cast (with some pitfall characters)
    • Unpredictable boot order from aggressive gameplay
    • Satisfying narrative payoff from twists and cast interactions

  3. Survivor: Winners at War (Season 40)

    • All-star cast with doubled stakes leads to dynamic gameplay
    • High-octane strategy with nowhere to hide
    • Well thought-out story telling and character arcs
    • Unfortunate old-school boot order

  4. Survivor: Heroes vs Villains

    • Epic old-school challenges we won't ever see again
    • Returning favorites cast with a clever theme
    • Dramatic strategic gameplay setting the stage for future Survivor eras

  5. Survivor: Cambodia - Second Chance (Season 31)

    • Twists that all actually worked out when a lot don't
    • Full cast of hard-ball players
    • Crazy over-the-top tribal councils you could never predict
    • Boring challenges make it last on my list
